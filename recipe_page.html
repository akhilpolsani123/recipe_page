Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed. Optionally add water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs, tilting the pan to ensure even coverage. Let the eggs cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Once the eggs set, add your fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute before serving.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with extra seasoning if needed.
